Rules

{{ if eq .Title "Calculator" }}

The expression must contain at least two digits and one operator, may contain multiple parentheses, the operands may be negative and non-integer, and the operator may be one of -, +, /, *.

{{ else if eq .Title "Settings" }}

The value of the run time must be specified as an integer number in seconds. Any non-integer, negative and zero numbers will not be accepted.

{{ else if or (eq .Title "Register") (eq .Title "Profile") }}

The password must contain at least one digit, at least one lower case character, at least one upper case character and at least one special character. The length of the password must be no less than 8 and no more than 32 characters.
